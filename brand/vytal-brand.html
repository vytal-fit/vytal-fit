Usage

✓ Do
  • Pick the lockup that fits the background: light vs dark.
  • Keep clear space ≈ the tile’s corner radius around the logo.
  • Let the pulse breathe; the green tile is the constant.
✕ Don’t
  • Recolor the tile or the pulse, or add gradients.
  • Stretch, rotate, or outline the lockup.
  • Use the signal colors (cyan/amber/red) as the brand.
